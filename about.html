From Voice to Code

My journey from a speech-language pathologist to code junkie

Coming from a successful career in speech-language pathology, Kaci has a unique perspective of accessibility in technology.

She currently works as a freelance web developer and accessibility consultant, curating custom websites from scratch that are both accessible and fun.

She has completed certified trainings in both UX/UI Design and UX writing, supplementing her knowledge on accessibility in technology with current research to give her designs a competitive edge.

Kaci has developed hands-on experience working in technology through her contributions with open-source projects, such as Mozilla Thunderbird, where she works closely with the accessibility community and UX team
