Bridging Clinical Speech Science and Technology

With a background in speech-language pathology, Kaci combines clinical expertise with front-end development and accessibility consulting.

She has completed certified trainings in both UX/UI Design and UX writing, supplementing her knowledge on accessibility in technology with current research to give her designs a competitive edge.

She contributes to Mozilla open-source accessibility projects such as Thunderbird's UX design team, participates in Accessibility Standards and Compliance Committee, designs inclusive user experiences, and applies her knowledge of speech science to AI and speech recognition technologies.
