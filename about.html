Bridging Clinical Speech Science and Technology

With 7+ years of clinical experience as a Speech-Language Pathologist, Kaci brings a uniquely human-centered foundation to digital accessibility — grounded in evidence-based practice, assistive technology, and a deep understanding of how people communicate, process information, and interact with technology.

She specializes in accessibility auditing, WCAG 2.1/2.2 compliance, and inclusive design consultation, translating technical standards into practical remediation strategies that improve usability for screen reader, keyboard, and assistive technology users. She is currently pursuing her Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies (CPAC) certification through IAAP (expected May 2026).

Kaci contributes to Thunderbird's open-source accessibility initiative, conducting manual and tool-assisted audits against WCAG and POUR principles and delivering developer-ready recommendations. She also serves on Thunderbird's Accessibility Standards and Compliance Committee, contributing to inclusive design governance across UX and engineering teams.
