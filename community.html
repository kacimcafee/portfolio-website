Southern Colorado Women in Technology

How It Started

At the beginning of my journey into tech, I felt isolated — and I didn't think I could be the only one. I reached out on social media to connect with other women in tech in the Colorado Springs area, and the response was immediate. A community of women who shared my experience quickly came together, and that momentum led to the founding of Southern Colorado Women in Technology.

How It's Going

The community is growing steadily, and the energy in the room reflects it. Members meet on the first Saturday of each month to collaborate, share ideas, and connect with women across a wide range of skills and experience.

How to Support

If you know someone who might be interested, spread the word — new members are always welcome. The more voices and perspectives we bring in, the stronger our community becomes.
