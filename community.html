Southern Colorado Women in Technology

How It Started

At the beginning of my journey into tech, I felt isolated despite the rich resources at my fingertips that previous women didn't have the luxury of having. I knew I couldn't be the only one, so I attempted to reach out to other women in tech local to the Colorado Springs area through social media. To my surprise, it was an instant hit â€” I found a community who often felt the same way I did. This led to the founding of Southern Colorado Women in Technology.

How It's Going

While still a small group, our little community is growing strong. We meet on the first Saturday of the month to collaborate and network with other women in the field with diverse skillsets.

How to Support

If you know someone who would be interested in joining SoCo WIT, spread the word! We are always looking to diversify our group with strong women.
